About Me

I'm Haseeb Ali, an Entry-Level SOC Analyst based in Pakistan, actively working toward my first role. I have been passionate about cybersecurity since childhood.

I hold the Google Cybersecurity Certificate and have built a portfolio of 14 security projects covering SQL-based log analysis, threat analysis, incident response, network traffic analysis, and risk assessment across frameworks including NIST CSF, PASTA, PCI DSS, GDPR and HIPAA.

I have 7+ years of Linux experience and a background in Python and SQL.

I enjoy working in the terminal, exploring new tools, and continuously pushing my understanding forward. I'm currently pursuing CompTIA Security+ and open to roles where I can grow, contribute, and make systems safer.
