01 — Overview

In this project, I built a home SOC lab to simulate real-world attack techniques mapped to the MITRE ATT&CK framework and evaluated how well Wazuh detects them out of the box.

The goal was not just to run attacks, but to think like a detection engineer: identify what the SIEM catches, find the gaps, and understand what it would take to close them.
