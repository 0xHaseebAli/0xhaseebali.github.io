Notes  
The bank operates in a coastal area with low crime rates but faces various internal and  
external risks due to its operations. With 100 on-premise and 20 remote employees, the  
human element increases exposure to social engineering threats like business email  
compromise. Data is handled by numerous individuals, systems, and doing business with  
other companies might increase the risks to data since it presents another way for the  
information to be compromised.  
Asset: The asset at risk of being harmed, damaged, or stolen.  
Risk(s): A potential risk to the organization's information systems and data.  
Description: A vulnerability that might lead to a security incident.  
Likelihood: Score from 1-3 of the chances of a vulnerability being exploited. A 1 means there's a low  
likelihood, a 2 means there's a moderate likelihood, and a 3 means there's a high likelihood. Severity:  
Score from 1-3 of the potential damage the threat would cause to the business. A 1 means a low  
severity impact, a 2 is a moderate severity impact, and a 3 is a high severity impact. Priority: How  
quickly a risk should be addressed to avoid the potential incident. Use the following formula to  
calculate the overall score: Likelihood x Impact Severity = Risk
