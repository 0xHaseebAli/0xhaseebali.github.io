Recommendations:  
This section outlines actionable recommendations for controls and compliance  
measures that your IT manager can communicate to stakeholders. Implementing these  
strategies will help mitigate risks to organizational assets and strengthen Botium Toys’  
overall security posture.  
To swiftly enhance security and safeguard sensitive information, the following  
measures should be prioritized:  
Principle of Least Privilege – Restrict user access to only the necessary resources.  
Separation of Duties – Distribute critical tasks among multiple employees to prevent  
conflicts of interest and reduce fraud risks.  
Intrusion Detection Systems (IDS) – Deploy monitoring tools to detect and respond to  
potential threats in real time.  
Disaster Recovery Plans – Establish and test robust procedures to ensure business  
continuity in case of disruptions.  
Strong Password Policies – Enforce complex passwords and multi-factor  
authentication (MFA) to prevent unauthorized access.  
Data Encryption – Protect sensitive data at rest and in transit to maintain  
confidentiality.  
By adopting these measures, Botium Toys can significantly reduce vulnerabilities and  
align with industry best practices for cybersecurity.
